I used this prototype to cut small pieces of primarily 1/2" wide, 1/8" thick aluminum strips, but it can be used for other materials, if a longer blade is used. Regardless, this would probably be a "detail" tool, such as a scroll saw, rather than a "quantity" tool, such as a circular saw.
 
This could be used to cut small square or round stock of various materials.
 
Small PVC, copper, or other pipe could easily be precision cut with the Jigsaw Miter Box.
 
While I'm sure there are many uses for this product, I can't think of any others at this point in time.
